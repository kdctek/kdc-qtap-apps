# qTap Finance — Parent Explainer Video Script

**Title:** "School Fees, Finally Simple."
**Duration:** ~1 min 50 sec
**Audience:** School parents
**Tone:** Warm, witty, reassuring. Not corporate. Think: a friend who just figured this out and is telling you about it.
**Music:** Light, upbeat acoustic (ukulele + soft percussion). Builds gently. Never overpowers the VO.

## PRODUCTION NOTES

**Total VO word count:** ~240 words (~1:50 at conversational pace)

### Voice
- Male or female, 30-40 years old
- Warm, conversational, Indian-English accent preferred
- Not a "movie trailer voice" — more like a smart friend explaining something over chai
- Pacing: relaxed but not slow. Confident pauses between beats.

### Visual Style
- Clean, modern flat illustration or screen recordings on phone mockups
- Colour palette: Blue (#2563EB), Green (#16A34A), White, with Yellow accents
- All UI shown should match the actual qTap Finance frontend (fee cards, badges, summary bar)
- Phone mockup should be centered, slightly angled for depth

### Music & SFX
- **0:00–0:12:** No music. Just ambient SFX (queue sounds, phone ringing) for the "problem" beat
- **0:13–1:50:** Light acoustic track. Builds slightly at 0:34 (payment section). Peaks at 1:43 (tagline). Fades on end card.
- **SFX cues:** Chime (0:12), satisfying "ding" on payment confirmation (1:12), subtle whoosh on screen transitions

### Deliverables
- **Landscape (16:9)** — YouTube, website embed
- **Portrait (9:16)** — Instagram Reels, WhatsApp Status
- **Square (1:1)** — Facebook, LinkedIn

### Subtitles
- Hardcoded subtitles recommended (many parents watch on mute in school WhatsApp groups)
- White text, dark semi-transparent background, large readable font
