## 1. What is the Staff Console?

The Staff Console is a website page where you manage student fees for your school. Think of it as your digital fee register.

**With the Staff Console, you can:**

- Search for any student and see their fee details
- Add a new student to the system
- Record payments when a parent pays fees in person (cash, UPI, cheque, etc.)
- View and search payment receipts
- Generate and download fee reports as Excel files

> **You do NOT need to install any app.** The Staff Console works in any browser — Chrome, Safari, or any other — on your phone, tablet, or computer.

## 2. Opening the Staff Console

### Step 1: Open your browser

Open **Chrome**, **Safari**, or any browser on your phone or computer.

### Step 2: Go to the Staff Console page

In the address bar at the top, type the Staff Console URL provided by your school (e.g., `yourschool.edu.in/staff`).

Then press **Enter**.

> **Tip:** Bookmark this page so you can open it with one tap every day. On Chrome, tap the star icon in the address bar to bookmark.

## 5. Adding a New Student

When a new student joins your school, follow these two steps: **create the account**, then **add enrollment**.

---

### Step 1: Create the Student Account

1. On the **Overview** tab, click the **"Create New"** button.
2. A form will appear with these fields:

| Field | Required? | What to enter | Example |
|:------|:---------:|:--------------|:--------|
| **Full Name** | Yes (marked with red *) | Student's or parent's full name | Rajesh Kumar |
| **Email** | No | Email address if available | rajesh@gmail.com |
| **Mobile** | No | Mobile number with country code | +919876543210 |

> **Important:** You must fill in at least one — either Email OR Mobile. Both are not required, but at least one is needed.

3. Click **"Create User"**.
4. The system will create the account and take you directly to the student's profile.

### Step 2: Add Enrollment (Assign Grade, Fees)

After creating the student, you need to **enroll them** in an academic year:

1. On the student's profile page, look for **"Add Enrollment"**.
2. Fill in:

| Field | What to select |
|:------|:---------------|
| **Academic Year** | The current year (e.g., 2025-2026) |
| **Grade** | The class/grade (e.g., Grade 1, Grade 5, etc.) |

| **RTE / Exempt** | Check this ONLY if the student is under RTE or exempt from fees |

3. Click **"Save"** or **"Add Enrollment"**.

### Understanding RTE / Exempt

| Term | What it means |
|:-----|:--------------|
| **RTE** (Right to Education) | Student admitted under the government RTE scheme. Fees are waived. |
| **Exempt** | Student is exempt from paying fees for any other reason (scholarship, special case, etc.) |

When a student is marked RTE or Exempt, their status shows in **green** and no payment is expected.

## 6. Finding an Existing Student

To find a student who is already in the system:

### Step by step

1. Go to the **Overview** tab.
2. In the search box, type any of the following:

| Search by | Example |
|:----------|:--------|
| Student's name (full or partial) | "Rajesh" or "Raj" |
| Parent's mobile number | "9876543210" |
| Email address | "rajesh@gmail.com" |

3. Click **"Find"** or press **Enter**.
4. A list of matching results will appear showing: **Name**, **Email**, and **User ID**.
5. Click on the student's name to open their profile.

## 7. Recording an Offline Payment

When a parent pays fees in person (by cash, UPI, cheque, etc.), you need to record it in the system.

---

### 7.1 Find the Student and Open Their Profile

1. Search for the student (see Section 6).
2. Click on the student's name to open their profile.

---

### 7.2 Find the Fee Slab to Record Payment For

1. On the student's profile, scroll down to the **enrollment card** for the current academic year.
2. You will see a table with columns: **Fee Slab**, **Amount Due**, **Paid**, **Balance**, **Due Date**, **Status**, **Actions**.
3. Find the fee slab the parent is paying for (e.g., "Tuition Fee").
4. Click the **"Record Payment"** button in the Actions column.

> **Note:** The "Record Payment" button only appears if there is a **balance due** and the student is **not** marked RTE/Exempt.

### 7.3 Fill in the Payment Form

A pop-up form titled **"Record Payment"** will appear. At the top, you will see a summary:

- **Student:** (name)
- **Academic Year:** (e.g., 2025-2026)
- **Grade:** (e.g., Grade 5)
- **Fee Slab:** (e.g., Tuition Fee)
- **Balance Due:** (shown in red, e.g., Rs 25,000)

Now fill in the form fields:

---

#### Field 1: Amount (Required)

| | |
|:--|:--|
| **What to enter** | The exact amount the parent is paying |
| **Example** | 5000 (no currency symbol needed) |
| **Partial payment?** | Yes! Enter whatever amount was paid. The balance will update automatically. |

#### Field 2: Payment Date (Required)

| | |
|:--|:--|
| **Default** | Today's date (auto-filled) |
| **Change it?** | Only if the payment was received on a different day |

#### Field 3: Payment Mode (Required)

Select how the parent paid:

| Option | When to select |
|:-------|:---------------|
| **Cash** | Parent paid in cash |
| **UPI** | Parent paid via Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc. |
| **Bank Transfer / NEFT / RTGS** | Parent paid through bank transfer |
| **Cheque** | Parent paid by cheque |
| **Credit/Debit Card** | Parent paid by card |
| **Other** | Any other method |

#### Field 4: UTR / Transaction ID (Optional)

| | |
|:--|:--|
| **What to enter** | The transaction reference number |
| **For UPI** | Enter the UPI transaction ID or UTR number |
| **For Bank Transfer** | Enter the NEFT/RTGS reference number |
| **For Cheque** | Enter the cheque number |
| **For Cash** | Leave blank |

#### Field 5: Payee Name (Optional)

| | |
|:--|:--|
| **What to enter** | Name of the person who actually paid |
| **When to fill** | Only if someone other than the registered parent/student is paying |
| **Otherwise** | Leave blank — the system will use the name already on file |

#### Field 6: Receipt / Proof (Optional)

| | |
|:--|:--|
| **What to upload** | A photo or scan of the payment receipt |
| **Accepted formats** | Images (JPG, PNG) or PDF |
| **Maximum size** | 2 MB |
| **How to upload** | Click the upload area and select the file from your phone/computer |

#### Field 7: Notes (Optional)

| | |
|:--|:--|
| **What to enter** | Any extra information about this payment |
| **Example** | "Partial payment, remaining next month" or "Paid by uncle" |

#### Field 8: Generate WooCommerce Order (Checkbox)

| | |
|:--|:--|
| **Default** | Checked (turned on) |
| **What it does** | Creates a proper receipt/order in the system |
| **Should you change it?** | No — keep it checked. Only uncheck if the administrator tells you to. |

### 7.4 Save the Payment

1. **Review** all the information you entered.
2. Click **"Save Payment"**.
3. To cancel without saving, click **"Cancel"**.

**After saving, the system will automatically:**

| What happens | Details |
|:-------------|:--------|
| Payment is recorded | A transaction entry is created with all your details |
| Balance updates | The fee slab balance reduces by the paid amount |
| Status changes | Changes to **"Paid"** (full amount) or **"Partial"** (partial amount) |
| Receipt created | A receipt/order is generated in the system |
| Your name is logged | The system records which staff member recorded this payment |

## 12. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

---

### Payment Questions

<details>
<summary><strong>Q: What if I entered the wrong amount?</strong></summary>

Contact the administrator immediately. They can edit or delete the payment transaction and you can re-record the correct amount.

**Do NOT try to fix it by recording another payment.** This will show double payment.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Q: Can I record a partial payment?</strong></summary>

**Yes!** Enter the amount that was actually paid. The system will:
- Update the balance automatically
- Mark the status as **"Partial"**

When the remaining amount is paid later, record another payment for the rest.

**Example:** If Tuition Fee is Rs 25,000 and parent pays Rs 10,000 now:
- Enter Amount: 10000
- Balance will show: Rs 15,000
- Status will change to: Partial
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Q: What if I selected the wrong payment mode?</strong></summary>

Contact the administrator after saving. They can edit the transaction details.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Q: A parent paid for multiple fee slabs at once. How do I record it?</strong></summary>

Record **each fee slab separately**. For example, if a parent paid Rs 33,000 for Tuition (Rs 25,000) + Transport (Rs 8,000):
1. Record Rs 25,000 against Tuition Fee
2. Record Rs 8,000 against Transport Fee

This keeps the records accurate for each fee category.
</details>

---

### Student Search Questions

<details>
<summary><strong>Q: I can't find a student. What should I do?</strong></summary>

Try these steps in order:
1. Search by **mobile number** instead of name
2. Search by **email** address
3. Try a **shorter name** (e.g., "Prat" instead of "Prateek")
4. Check for **spelling variations** (e.g., "Pratik" vs "Prateek")
5. If still not found, ask the administrator — the student may not be registered yet
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Q: I accidentally created a duplicate student. What do I do?</strong></summary>

Contact the administrator. They can merge or delete duplicate accounts. **Do NOT add enrollment to the duplicate account.**
</details>

---

### Dashboard Questions

<details>
<summary><strong>Q: What does "Pending Verifications" mean on the dashboard?</strong></summary>

These are offline payments that have been recorded but not yet verified/approved by the administrator.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Q: The numbers on the dashboard look wrong. What happened?</strong></summary>

Check that the correct **Academic Year** is selected in the dropdown. The stats show data only for the selected year.
</details>

---

### Login Questions

<details>
<summary><strong>Q: I'm not receiving the WhatsApp OTP. What should I do?</strong></summary>

1. Make sure your phone has WhatsApp installed and working
2. Check your internet connection
3. Wait 30 seconds and tap "Send OTP" again
4. If it still doesn't arrive, contact the administrator
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Q: How do I log out?</strong></summary>

Go to the main site menu and look for the **Log Out** option. You can also close the browser tab, but you will remain logged in for next time.
</details>

---

### Report Questions

<details>
<summary><strong>Q: What's the difference between Term-wise and Month-wise reports?</strong></summary>

| | Term-wise | Month-wise |
|:--|:----------|:-----------|
| **Groups by** | Academic terms (Term 1, Term 2, Term 3) | Calendar months (April, May, June...) |
| **Best for** | Seeing fee collection by school terms | Seeing monthly collection trends |
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Q: Can I print the report directly?</strong></summary>

It's better to **download the Excel file** first, then print from Excel or Google Sheets. This gives you more control over the print layout.
</details>

## 13. Troubleshooting

| Problem | Possible Cause | Solution |
|:--------|:---------------|:---------|
| Page won't load | Slow internet | Check your internet connection and refresh the page |
| "No permission" message | Account not set up as staff | Contact administrator to grant staff access |
| OTP not arriving | WhatsApp issue | Check WhatsApp is installed; try sending OTP again |
| Record Payment button missing | No balance due, or student is RTE/Exempt | Check if the fee is already paid or student is exempt |
| Report shows no data | Wrong academic year selected | Change the Academic Year dropdown |
| Search shows no results | Spelling mistake or student not registered | Try different search terms or contact administrator |
| File upload fails | File too large | Make sure the file is under 2 MB |
| Page looks broken | Old browser | Update your browser or try Chrome |
